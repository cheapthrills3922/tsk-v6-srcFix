Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25/03/25

At Toursketchers, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website, services, and travel-related offerings.

  1. 1. Information We Collect

    We may collect the following types of information:
    • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address, passport details, and payment information when you book a trip
    • Travel Preferences: Destination choices, accommodation preferences, and special requests
    • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and cookies for website analytics and improved user experience.

  2. 2. How We Use Your Information

    We use your information to:
    • Process bookings, reservations, and payments.
    • Provide customer support and travel assistance.
    • Personalize travel recommendations and offers.
    • Improve our website and services.
    • Send promotional emails and updates (you may opt out anytime).

  3. 3. How We Protect Your Information

    We implement security measures such as encryption, secure servers, and restricted access to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

  4. 4. Sharing Your Information

    We do not sell or rent your data. However, we may share your information with:
    • Travel partners (hotels, airlines, tour operators) for booking purposes.
    • Payment processors to complete transactions securely.
    • Legal authorities, if required by law or to protect our rights.

  5. 5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    Our website uses cookies to improve user experience and analyze site traffic. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

  6. 6. Your Rights and Choices

    You have the right to:
    • Access, update, or delete your personal data
    • Opt out of marketing emails
    • Request information on how your data is processed.

  7. 7. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date."

  8. 8. Contact Us

    For any privacy-related concerns, contact us at:
    • Email: info@toursketchers.com
