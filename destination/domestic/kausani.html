Kausani

Description

Kausani is a village in the Bageshwar district of Kumaon Mandal in Uttarakhand, India, located in Garur Tehsil. Kausani, a beautiful mountain tourist destination, lies 53 kilometers north of Almora district. It stands atop the Himalayas of Pingnath, offering a magnificent view of the Nanda Devi mountain. Situated between the Kosi and Gomati rivers, Kausani is often called the “Switzerland of India.” Its beautiful scenic views, sports, and religious places attract tourists.

Best Time to Visit Kausani

You can visit Kausani at any time throughout the year. However, the best time to visit is during the summer season, as visitors can enjoy the pleasant atmosphere during this time.

How to Reach Kausani

  • By Air - The nearest airport is Pantnagar Airport. You can easily get a taxi or bus from the airport to reach Kausani, which is 170 km away.
  • By Rail - The nearest railway junction is Kathgodam, from where you can take a bus or taxi to Kausani, which is 135 km away.
  • By Road - Buses run regularly from Delhi's ISBT Anand Vihar bus stand to Kausani. Other districts of the state can also be reached by bus to Kausani.
